Welcome to the Kerala Association - Dallas

The Kerala Association Of Dallas was established in 1976 as a non-profit cultural association to promote and foster international relationship and goodwill. This Association is the member of the Federation of Kerala Associations in North America (FOKANA) and also is a member of National Federation of Indian-American Association (NFIA). More About Us.
